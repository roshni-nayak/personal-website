What is Setu AA?

Account aggregators (AAs) are RBI-regulated entities that enable companies to collect financial data from their customers through APIs. Companies can use Setu’s AA APIs in their app, and offer the smoothest onboarding experience for their customers.

Some of my contributions

  • Collaborated with PMs, designers, researchers, and engineers to envision, design, and create the app and UX flows. I explored the current financial data sharing space in India and focused on creating the app experience such that it is accessible to all Indians.
  • Gathered contextual research on competitors, read and analysed past user reports to understand user behaviors and patterns.
  • Explored different solutions through wireframing and brainstorming, and then proceeded to creating user flows, concept sketches and high-level mocks that I delivered to my team. Since it was an iterative process, my team and I conducted usability tests too, and then made changes based on the feedback we received.
  • I was particularly involved in creating the website as well as the version 2 upgrades of the Setu AA app.
